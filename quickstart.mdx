This guide takes you from your first sign-in to a result you can act on. You do
not need to configure anything to get value on day one.

## Before you begin

You need:

- A NEXOS CORE account provided by Horizon Managed Services
- Access to [nexus.horizonmanaged.com](https://nexus.horizonmanaged.com)

<Note>
  Don't have an account yet? Contact
  [support@horizonmanaged.com](mailto:support@horizonmanaged.com) and we'll get
  you set up.
</Note>

## Your first result

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Open [nexus.horizonmanaged.com](https://nexus.horizonmanaged.com) and sign
    in with your Horizon Managed Services credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your numbers">
    Your dashboard opens to the metrics that matter — the health of the
    business at a glance, not a wall of raw data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on an insight">
    Follow a recommendation to its source, then take the next step the platform
    suggests. That's the loop: data, to insight, to a decision you can defend.
  </Step>
</Steps>
